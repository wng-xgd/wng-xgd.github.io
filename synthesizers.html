DELIRIUM·FM — Experimental FM Synth for Reaktor

DELIRIUM·FM is a hand-designed FM synthesizer built in Reaktor 6, featuring two carrier oscillators, four modulators, a dedicated LFO, and an integrated FX section for creating dark, metallic, and atmospheric sounds. The interface is fully hand-drawn, giving the instrument a raw, distressed character that matches its sound palette.
Features：
2 Carrier Oscillators (Sine / Saw / Pulse / Imp)
4 Modulating Oscillators with resonant filters.
Dedicated 5-stage LFO
Built-in FX: Feedback, Diffusion, Delay, EQ, Filters
Snapshot system with 10 custom presets
Works inside any DAW through Reaktor
